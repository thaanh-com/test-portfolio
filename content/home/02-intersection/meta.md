title: "Alternative Vehicle Controls inspired by Bicycle Handlebars"
---
2021
Intersection
Rapid Prototyping

An automotive control system prototype was created using Arduino and designed after bicycle handlebars. Acceleration is controlled by pulling the grips inward, braking by pushing them outward, and steering by tilting the handlebar, emulating intuitive cyclist’s motion.
