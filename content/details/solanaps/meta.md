Sunrise simulation device for sleep improvement
Product Design and UI

Our team’s research revealed a common preference for visual over auditory wake-up cues. Solanap took this preference and transferred it into a sleep improvement tool. A custom head device simulates sunrise with soft light and gently wakes up its user. It helps users align naps and night sleep with their natural REM cycles, leading to better sleep quality.
