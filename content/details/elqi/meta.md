App fostering mental wellbeing
UI and UX

Elqi is an MVP app designed for improving mental wellbeing and supporting healthy relationships. It is intended to transform addictive app habits into mindful breathing exercises. The design is tailored for rapid development and scalability, ensuring a cohesive and adaptable design system.
